Curriculum Overview

Our curriculum is thoughtfully designed to be comprehensive and balanced, following the guidelines of the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE). We aim to provide a robust educational foundation while fostering intellectual curiosity and a passion for lifelong learning. The curriculum integrates core academic subjects with a rich array of co-curricular and extra-curricular activities to ensure the holistic development of each student.

Pedagogical Approach

Our teaching methodology is student-centric, interactive, and inquiry-based. We move beyond rote learning to foster critical thinking, problem-solving, and creativity. Our pedagogical tools include:

  • Smart classrooms with digital learning aids.
  • Project-based and experiential learning.
  • Collaborative group work and peer learning.
  • Regular assessments and personalized feedback.
We believe in creating an engaging and supportive atmosphere where students feel empowered to ask questions and explore new ideas.

Extra-Curricular Activities

Education at Patkai extends beyond the classroom. We offer a diverse range of extra-curricular activities to nurture the hidden talents of our students. These programs are designed to enhance creativity, physical fitness, and teamwork, providing students with opportunities to explore their interests and develop into well-rounded individuals.

Sports

Fostering teamwork and physical fitness through activities like football, basketball, and athletics.

Creative Arts

Unleashing creativity with programs in music, dance, drama, and various fine arts disciplines.

Eco Club

Promoting environmental awareness and sustainability through hands-on projects and campaigns.

Science Club

Igniting curiosity for discovery with exciting experiments, workshops, and science fairs.

Debate Club

Developing critical thinking and public speaking skills through structured debates and discussions.

Literary Society

Celebrating literature with reading sessions, creative writing workshops, and poetry slams.
