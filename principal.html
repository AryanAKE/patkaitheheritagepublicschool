Educational Philosophy

"At Patkai The Heritage Public School, we are committed to holistic development, ensuring that academics, values, and life skills grow hand in hand."

I believe in creating an environment where every child is recognized as unique. By providing personalized attention within our modern infrastructure and maintaining a low student-teacher ratio, we give young minds the freedom to grow without undue pressure. My focus is on aligning our curriculum with the NEP 2020 to foster critical thinking and creativity.
