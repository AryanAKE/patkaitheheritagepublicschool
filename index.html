Principal's Desk

Mr. Pulok Banerjee

At Patkai The Heritage Public School, we believe in nurturing every child through holistic development, ensuring that academics, values, and life skills grow hand in hand. Our well-motivated team of teachers, supported by a low student-teacher ratio, provides personalised attention to every learner. With spacious classrooms, smart teachers conducting smart classes, and a well-built infrastructure, we create an environment where learning is engaging, meaningful, and future-ready. Our scholastic and co-scholastic programs are thoughtfully designed in alignment with NEP 2020, giving young minds the space to grow freely without any undue pressure. We offer proper sports and cultural facilities, along with personality enhancement programs that help shape confident and responsible citizens. At Patkai, we recognise and nurture every student's unique abilities, offering bonus credit points for co-curricular participation and taking special care to identify and develop individual talents. Together, we strive to build a community where every child feels inspired to learn, explore, and excel.

WHY PATKAI?

At Patkai The Heritage Public School, we offer a unique educational experience that combines academic excellence with cultural preservation. Our holistic approach to education ensures that students develop not only intellectually but also emotionally and socially.

We provide state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, and a nurturing environment that encourages creativity, critical thinking, and leadership skills. Our commitment to preserving and honoring our heritage while preparing students for the future sets us apart. We believe in creating global citizens who are proud of their roots and ready to make a positive impact on the world.
