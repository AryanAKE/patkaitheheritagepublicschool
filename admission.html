Admissions

Admission Overview

Welcome to Patkai The Heritage Public School. We are delighted that you are considering our institution for your child's educational journey. Our admission process is designed to be transparent and straightforward, ensuring that prospective students and their families feel supported every step of the way. We seek to admit students who are eager to learn, demonstrate a positive attitude, and are willing to contribute to our vibrant school community.

Admissions are open for classes Nursery through IX and for Class XI, subject to the availability of seats. We encourage you to visit our campus to experience our welcoming environment and state-of-the-art facilities firsthand.

Admission Procedure

Follow these steps to complete the admission process:

  1. Obtain Application Form
    Collect the form from the school office or download it from our website.
  2. Form Submission
    Submit the completed application form along with the required documents.
  3. Entrance Assessment
    Applicants for Class I and above will undergo a basic assessment test.
  4. Interaction
    A formal interaction with the Principal/Admission Committee will be scheduled.
  5. Admission Offer
    Successful candidates will receive an admission offer and further instructions.
