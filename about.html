About Our School

Our Roots

Foundational History

A Legacy of Vision

Patkai The Heritage Public School was founded in [Year] by a group of visionary educators and community leaders who recognized the need for an institution that blends academic rigor with a deep appreciation for our cultural heritage. Their dream was to create a learning sanctuary where students could receive a modern education without losing touch with their roots. The school started with a humble batch of 50 students and has since grown into a beacon of knowledge and culture in Assam, nurturing thousands of young minds.

Our Guiding Principles

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide quality education that nurtures intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and moral values. We are dedicated to creating a dynamic learning environment that encourages students to explore their potential, celebrate diversity, and preserve our rich cultural heritage for future generations.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution that harmoniously combines traditional values with modern pedagogy. We aspire to cultivate confident, compassionate, and responsible individuals who are equipped to excel in a globalized world while remaining firmly rooted in their heritage.

IAt Patkai The Heritage Public School, we believe in nurturing every child through holistic development, ensuring that academics, values, and life skills grow hand in hand. Together, we strive to build a community where every child feels inspired to learn, explore, and excel.

Our Facilities

Infrastructure

We provide state-of-the-art facilities to support a comprehensive educational experience. Our campus includes spacious and well-ventilated classrooms, advanced science and computer labs, a well-stocked library, a dedicated arts and music wing, and extensive sports facilities including a playground and basketball court. We believe that a world-class infrastructure is essential for nurturing well-rounded individuals.
